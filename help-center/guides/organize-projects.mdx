## Overview

Keep documentation neat by organizing into different projects and folders.

## Steps

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create a new project">
    ````
    From the dashboard, click "New Project" and fill out the info.
    ````
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add folders">
    ````
    In your project, click "Add Folder" to group related docs.
    ````
  </Step>
  <Step title="Move docs into folders">
    ````
    Drag and drop pages into folders as needed.
    ````
  </Step>
</Steps>
